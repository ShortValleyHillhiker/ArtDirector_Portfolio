FourTwoSix Media

Visual identity for a Munich-based production company specializing in sports cinematography and branded content.

  • Brand identity

More Less Information

FourTwoSix is a visual production studio that focuses on photorealistic CGI, high-end drone footage, and immersive content, the studio brings narrative and visual precision to everything from social campaigns to branded shorts.

Its existing identity no longer reflected the quality and ambition of its work. Despite producing content that consistently outperformed expectations, the brand lacked a cohesive system that could communicate its attention to detail, technical expertise, and sense of storytelling. FourTwoSix needed an identity that felt as intentional and crafted as the work it puts on screen.!

The rebrand centers on a modular 2x6 grid, both a structural foundation and symbolic reference to the studio’s name. Within this grid, a simple inversion creates a distinct visual mark: abstract, minimal, and unmistakably digital. This system serves as the backbone of the identity, framing content while hinting at the studio’s methodical approach to design.

A suite of dynamic gradients brings energy to the identity, evoking lens flares, cinematic lighting, and the fluidity of digital storytelling. These gradients create a mood, signal movement, and reflect the studios connection to light, texture, and depth. Whether used subtly or dramatically across full-screen video, they carry the weight and emotion of a FourTwoSix frame.

The result is a brand system that feels modern, cinematic, and deliberate. Every element, from typography to tonality, is built to support the core idea: making every frame count. FourTwoSix now has an identity that amplifies its work, signals its creative and technical capabilities, and positions it confidently for new opportunities.
