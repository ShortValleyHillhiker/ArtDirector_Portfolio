Vesta Arkitekter

Brand identity for Vesta, an architecture startup founded by two Chalmers graduates, whose work centers on architectural conservation & sustainable reuse.

  • Brand identity
  • Web design

More Less Information

Vesta Arkitekter is a tactile and thoughtful architecture studio rooted in the traditions of building preservation, material honesty and human-scale design. Working across restoration, new construction and interior architecture, Vesta’s practice bridges the past and the present with quiet precision.

AOE developed the visual identity, templates and brand tools for Vesta Arkitekter, crafting a system that balances the gravitas of historic preservation with the studio’s contemporary sensibilities.

Named after the Roman goddess of hearth and home, “Vesta” signals care, permanence and the lived experience of space. The brand centers on a material-first philosophy, where wood, stone and brick are not references but starting points.

The wordmark is calm and confident, with letterforms that echo the proportions of architectural drafting. Typography is set in a utilitarian serif and a neutral sans — a nod to both blueprints and classical inscriptions.

A modular grid system, derived from building plans, informs everything from printed matter to web layout. Thin line motifs echo technical drawings and load-bearing structures. The identity flexes between stillness and motion, using architectural gestures like sliding panels and scaling masses.

AOE created a suite of templates for project documentation, architectural proposals and portfolios. Each is designed to foreground the work, not decorate it — with space for scale, plans and storytelling.

The palette is drawn directly from the material world: rust red from painted timber, granite gray from Västkustens bedrock, pale white from limewashed walls. These colors appear subtly, like background textures, never overwhelming the work.
